# MILOS Life pillar two-axis grounding — migration complete

## Context

Third pillar pass after [[2026-04-25-milos-faith-grounding-complete]] and [[2026-04-25-milos-ummah-grounding-complete]]. Life carried 236 legacy-string `sources` entries. Inspection confirmed identical convention to Faith/Ummah; collection prefixes are a strict subset of Ummah's set (no new collections). ## Consequences

- Three pillars (Faith + Ummah + Life) now fully two-axis-compliant — 973 entries cleared (212 + 525 + 236). Strict-mode lint count dropped 1167 → 931.
- Parser proven on heterogeneous content — Physical Health, Mental Health, Safety boards all migrated cleanly. Strong evidence the legacy markdown convention is uniform across the seed corpus.
- Zero manual patches needed — migration → tests-green → audit was a single pass. Confirms the script-copy approach is mature enough to fan out the remaining four pillars (Intellect 236, Family 233, Wealth 236, Environment 226 — total 931) in parallel.
