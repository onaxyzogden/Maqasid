Islamic Life Operating System

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا اتَّقُوا اللَّهَ وَلْتَنظُرْ نَفْسٌ مَّا قَدَّمَتْ لِغَدٍ O you who believe, fear Allah. And let every soul look to what it has put forth for tomorrow. Surah Al-Hashr, 59:18

#  Your life is not seven separate problems.  
It is _one trust -- with seven dimensions._

Faith, life, intellect, family, wealth, environment, and ummah -- the seven Maqasid al-Shariah. Not a productivity system. Not a habit tracker. An operating system built on the premise that every domain of your life is an act of stewardship, answerable to the One who gave it to you. 

The Why Is This for Me? Who this is for, what it means, and why it exists.  ↓ The Structure See the Seven Pillars Seven dimensions of the Maqasid al-Shariah, each with its own module.  ↓

What this is for

##  When your deen has structure --  
_your dunya stops falling apart._

  * 01

Most Muslims manage their lives in fragments. Work in one system, money in another, family in the margins, faith in whatever time remains. The result is not laziness -- it is exhaustion from holding pieces that were never meant to be separate.

  * 02

The Maqasid al-Shariah are not abstract jurisprudence. They are the operating system your life already runs on -- whether you designed it that way or not. Faith, life, intellect, family, wealth, environment, ummah. Seven dimensions. One trust.

  * 03

MILOS does not add more to your plate. It gives your plate a shape. Every task, every goal, every commitment maps to a pillar. Every pillar has a state. You see the whole picture -- and you see where the imbalance is before it becomes a crisis.

  * 04

This is not about optimisation. It is about remembrance -- structured remembrance that your work serves a purpose beyond itself, and that the One who assigned the trust also provides the means to carry it.




The question at the centre

##  What if you could see your whole life  
as _one coherent trust_? 

Not a dashboard. Not a to-do list. A system that understands that your family is not separate from your faith, that your wealth is not separate from your environment, that your intellect is not separate from your ummah. Seven pillars. One operating system. Built on the premise that the Maqasid are not philosophy -- they are architecture. 

What MILOS is

##  A pillar system _and_ a life dashboard. 

Seven pillars, each with its own module, its own milestones, its own Islamic grounding. CeremonyGate marks the threshold of each domain with remembrance. ReadinessCheck surfaces what is incomplete. PrayerOverlay keeps salah visible. The BBOS pipeline runs inside it -- because your business is one of the seven, not the centre of them all.
