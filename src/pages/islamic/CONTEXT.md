# Islamic Pages — CONTEXT.md

## Purpose
Islamic knowledge pages -- educational and reference views for Quranic study, hadith exploration, the Five Pillars of Islam, and general Islamic scholarship. These are knowledge-focused pages, not pillar dashboards.
