# Prophet Tahajjud transition sunnah — NotebookLM round

Scope: Tahajjud Before (waking + qiyam entry) and After (witr adhkar + last-third du'a).

## Before Tahajjud (waking from sleep with intent of night prayer)

### 1. Wipe sleep + recite last 10 verses of Al-Imran (3:190-200)
**Bukhari 4569 / Muslim 763** — Ibn Abbas (RA) spent the night at his aunt Maymuna's house. When the Prophet ﷺ woke from sleep for Tahajjud, he wiped sleep from his face with his hand, then recited the last 10 verses of Surah Al-Imran. *(Grade: Sahih)*

**Quran 3:190** — "Indeed, in the creation of the heavens and the earth and the alternation of night and day are signs for those of understanding."

### 2. Wake-during-night du'a
**Bukhari 1154** — 'Ubadah ibn as-Samit narrated that whoever wakes at night and says: "La ilaha illallah wahdahu la sharika lah, lahul-mulku wa lahul-hamd, wa huwa 'ala kulli shay'in qadir. Alhamdulillah, wa subhan Allah, wa la ilaha illallah, wallahu akbar, wa la hawla wa la quwwata illa billah", then says "Allahumma-ghfir li" or makes du'a — it is answered; if he performs wudu and prays, his prayer is accepted. *(Grade: Sahih)*

### 3. Siwak before standing for Qiyam
**Bukhari 245 / Muslim 255** — 'Aishah (RA): "When the Prophet ﷺ rose at night (for Qiyam), he would clean his mouth with the siwak." *(Grade: Sahih)*

### 4. Tahajjud istiftah du'a
**Muslim 770** — 'Aishah (RA): "When the Prophet ﷺ stood for Tahajjud, he would open with: 'Allahumma rabba Jibrila wa Mika'ila wa Israfila, fatiras-samawati wal-ard, 'alimal-ghaybi wash-shahada, anta tahkumu bayna 'ibadika fima kanu fihi yakhtalifun, ihdini limakhtulifa fihi minal-haqqi bi-idhnik, innaka tahdi man tasha'u ila siratim-mustaqim.'" *(Grade: Sahih)*

## After Tahajjud / Witr (pre-dawn)

### 5. "Subhanal-Malikil-Quddus" ×3 after Witr
**Nasa'i 1733 / Abu Dawud 1430** — Ubayy ibn Ka'b (RA): the Prophet ﷺ, after the salam of Witr, would say "Subhanal-Malikil-Quddus" three times, lengthening the third. *(Grade: Sahih)*

### 6. Witr Qunut du'a
**Abu Dawud 1425 / Tirmidhi 464 / Nasa'i 1745** — al-Hasan ibn 'Ali (RA): the Prophet ﷺ taught me to say in Witr: "Allahumma-hdini fi man hadayt, wa 'afini fi man 'afayt, wa tawallani fi man tawallayt, wa barik li fima a'tayt, wa qini sharra ma qadayt, fa innaka taqdi wa la yuqda 'alayk, wa innahu la yadhillu man wallayt, wa la ya'izzu man 'adayt, tabarakta rabbana wa ta'alayt." *(Grade: Hasan-Sahih)*

### 7. Last-third-of-night du'a & istighfar
**Bukhari 1145 / Muslim 758** — Abu Hurairah (RA): the Prophet ﷺ said, "Our Lord descends each night to the lowest heaven when the last third of the night remains, saying: 'Who is calling upon Me that I may answer him? Who is asking of Me that I may grant him? Who is seeking My forgiveness that I may forgive him?'" *(Grade: Sahih)*
